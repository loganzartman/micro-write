μwrite

because μ need to write

Text is magically saved either on Google Drive or on your local machine. Note that μwrite is not designed for collaboration, and editing from multiple devices at once will cause conflicts. No worries, though--just delete whatever you don't want.

Features
  • Click the word count to zero it, or double-click to reset.
  • Select something to count words or zero based on the selection.
  • Click the sun to turn off the lights.
  • Click the sp to toggle spell check (requires reload).
  • Click the μ to read this again.
  • Fully supports unicode!
